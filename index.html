About Roblox

Roblox (formerly named DynaBlocks) is a safe multiplayer game open to all ages, overall it's a resourceful application and resembles lego-bricks. In this game, you can interact and chat with players from all around the world. You can enjoy the fun wheter you're on Android, Apple, Amazon, laptops, and even computers. A much powering imagination platform!

Features

  • Users can design their own games along with playing a wide variety of games.
  • The Developer Exchange, or DevEx, is a program that allows Roblox's top game developers to "cash-out" their Robux for real-world currency.
  • Roblox occasionally hosts real life and virtual events for players which are fun.

Why is Roblox awesome?

  1. Roblox takes online safety and privacy very seriously, therefore users under and above the age of 13 are secure. Click here to learn more about Roblox account safety & privacy.
  2. Roblox provides users with great resources to teach how to design game(s). For example, Lua is embedded in Roblox also used as the game creation programming language which you can learn for free, at any level.
  3. Roblox inspires creativity while also teaching the value of its community and teamwork.
